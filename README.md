# Professional-Office-Assistance-N8n
Professional Office Assistant – AI Email Responder

The Professional Office Assistant is an AI-powered system that automatically reads incoming emails and responds professionally based on the email’s category. It is designed to help automate workplace communication by generating clear, polite, and context-aware email replies.

Workflow
Step 1 — Receive Email
The system receives an incoming email at: hpadee01@gmail.com
This email may contain:
1)Meeting scheduling requests
2)Leave approval or application
3)Informational or notification messages

Step 2 — AI Agent Processes and Responds
The AI agent:
1)Reads the email body and subject
2)Identifies the email type
3)Generates a clear, professional, and context-appropriate reply

Sends the response back to the sender automatically
